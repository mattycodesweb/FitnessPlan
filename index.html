What to eat

Simple nutrition rules for losing belly fat while keeping muscle.

Protein target

Try to eat about 180–190 g of protein each day to support muscle while cutting fat.

Meal structure

Build meals around a lean protein, a smart carb, and vegetables. Examples: chicken + rice + broccoli, salmon + potatoes + salad, Greek yogurt + berries + oats.

Best foods

Eggs, chicken breast, turkey, tuna, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, beans, oats, fruit, potatoes, rice, leafy greens, nuts, olive oil.

Foods to limit

Cut back on sugary drinks, alcohol, fast food, pastries, and late-night snacking. Keep portions steady and consistent.
